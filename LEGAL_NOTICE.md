## Geographic Usage Restrictions

Horizon does not, cannot, and will not implement age verification systems or
parental control infrastructure. As a result of recent legislation imposing
these requirements on software developers, Horizon is not intended for use
in the following jurisdictions:

### In effect now

- **Brazil**: Digital ECA, Law 15.211/2025, effective March 17, 2026.
  Imposes age verification and parental control obligations on any technology
  product or service likely to be accessed by minors in Brazil, regardless of
  where the developer is located.

### Effective January 1, 2027

- **California, USA**: Digital Age Assurance Act (AB 1043). Requires
  operating system providers and app developers to implement and consume
  age verification APIs.

### Effective January 1, 2028 (if passed)

- **Colorado, USA**: Age Attestation on Computing Devices (SB26-051).
  Modeled on California AB 1043. Not yet passed as of March 2026; this
  restriction will take effect only if the bill is enacted.

These decisions are subject to change. This notice will be updated as the
legal landscape develops, including if legal challenges to these laws succeed
or if a privacy-preserving compliance path becomes available. As of now, the
Horizon developers do not have the legal capability to fulfill the demands
these laws put in place, and thus refuse to comply.
